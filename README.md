# BrainCore
"BrainCore transforms learning with interactive quizzes tailored to your curriculum. Track progress, earn badges, and master subjects through gamified challenges. Ideal for students, teachers, and lifelong learners."
Braincore is a modern quiz app designed to challenge your knowledge, expand your mind, and make learning fun. Whether you're a trivia enthusiast, a student, or just looking for a mental workout, Braincore has something for everyone.
✨ Features
Diverse Quiz Categories: From science to pop culture, history to technology
Timed Challenges: Test your speed and accuracy
Leaderboards: Compete with friends and global players
Custom Quizzes: Create and share your own quizzes
Progress Tracking: Earn badges and track your learning journey
Beautiful UI: Clean, intuitive design with animated transitions
Multi-Platform Support: iOS, Android, and Web
🚀 Getting Started
Prerequisites
Flutter 3.13+
Dart 3.1+
iOS/Android development environment (for mobile builds)
